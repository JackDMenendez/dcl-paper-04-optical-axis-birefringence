% ── Gauge sector: null polarization split (conditional on the blocks) ─
Gauge-sector null polarization split (birefringence cancels), \emph{conditional on the geometric blocks}
    & With $\boldsymbol\mu^{-1} = \gamma\operatorname{adj}(\boldsymbol\varepsilon)$ the photon dispersion is a \emph{double} transverse root $\det(K\boldsymbol\mu^{-1}K+\omega^2\boldsymbol\varepsilon) = \det(\boldsymbol\varepsilon)\,\omega^2(\omega^2-k^T\boldsymbol\varepsilon k)^2$ for every direction, so the split $\propto|\varepsilon_a\mu^{-1}_a - \varepsilon_p\mu^{-1}_p| = 0$
    & Isotropic-impedance product $\varepsilon_i(\mu^{-1})_i = \gamma\det\boldsymbol\varepsilon$; rescaling-invariant, so immune to the deferred normalization; holds for any hop vectors
    & \emph{Adjugate theorem} $Q_B = \operatorname{adj}(\boldsymbol\varepsilon)$, $\boldsymbol\varepsilon = MM^T$, proven for general vectors (\texttt{electric\_induced\_action.py}, symbolic). \emph{Engine + numeric:} \texttt{exp\_03\_geometric\_blocks} confirms the adjugate from the two engine blocks ($5\times10^{-15}$); the Fresnel solver \texttt{exp\_03\_dispersion\_core} gives $\max|v_+-v_-| = 1.6\times10^{-15}$ over 2000 directions (detuned control $0.5$); $2\times10^4$-$k$ symbolic-guard sweep $<10^{-14}$. An \emph{independent code-path verification} (same framework/engine), not a separate physical measurement.

% ── Gauge sector: blocks as the dynamical induced action ─────────────
Gauge blocks as the actual \emph{dynamical} induced action ($\boldsymbol\varepsilon$, $\boldsymbol\mu^{-1}$)
    & The two forms are the natural \emph{geometric} plaquette quadratic forms: $\boldsymbol\varepsilon = \sum_a\mathbf{V}_a\mathbf{V}_a^T$ (temporal holonomy $\Theta_a = \mathbf{V}_a\!\cdot\!\mathbf{E}$, eig $\{1,4,4\}$), $\boldsymbol\mu^{-1} = Q_B$ (spatial holonomy, eig $\{4,4,16\}$)
    & The dynamical induced action is a matter-integrated (loop-level) effective action; the plaquette forms are bare geometric actions
    & \emph{Vertex verified:} $\delta\phi = \omega+V$ read off \texttt{HopOperator.step} (\texttt{exp\_04\_electric\_permittivity\_extraction}; fidelity $4\times10^{-19}$, $\omega$ cancels, $a_t=1$). \emph{Gap (why PART):} this validates the field--matter \emph{vertex}, not the quadratic effective action $\Gamma^{(2)}$; a general $D_{3d}$-invariant form has two free coefficients, so the $1{:}4$ ratio is not symmetry-forced. Promotion needs $\varepsilon_{ij}\propto\partial^2\Gamma/\partial E_i\partial E_j$ ($\Gamma=-\log\det T$) or a measured 2nd-order response; the $\operatorname{Tr}\ln T$ route is Ward-divergent on the flat axis (naive band sum $\to$ wrong object about $(1,-1,0)$) and is a stated open follow-on.
    & \texttt{PART} \\

A \emph{speed} anisotropy, not birefringence, and distinct from the null split. Whether the fixed lattice or an averaged ensemble is physical is undecided.
